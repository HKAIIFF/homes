About HKAIIGF

關於HKAIIGF

The Hong Kong AI International Game Festival (HKAIIGF) is the world's first annual event dedicated to AI-native games. Through the trinity model of "Competitive Glory + Industry Incubation + Talent Cultivation," it builds a complete value chain from education to market, from creation to competition. With Hong Kong as the global hub, it aims to create the "Mecca" of the AI gaming industry.

香港AI國際遊戲節（HKAIIGF）是全球首個專注於AI原生遊戲的年度盛會，通過"賽事榮耀+產業孵化+人才培養"的三位一體模式，構建從教育到市場、從創作到競技的完整價值鏈。以香港為全球樞紐，打造AI遊戲產業的"麥加聖地"。

Frequently Asked Questions

常見問題

What are AI-native games?

什麼是AI原生遊戲？

AI-native games refer to games that deeply integrate AI technology in the design and development process, with AI participation not less than 40%. This includes AI-generated content, AI-driven NPCs, human-machine collaborative gameplay and other innovative forms.

AI原生遊戲是指在設計與開發過程中深度整合AI技術，AI參與度不低於40%的遊戲作品。這包括AI生成內容、AI驅動的NPC、人機協同玩法等創新形式。

How to participate in AIG Championship?

如何參加AIG冠軍賽？

There are two ways: 1) Participate in four Grand Slam tournaments to accumulate points, top 16 can enter Hong Kong Finals; 2) Win the Dark Horse Challenge at the Carnival to directly qualify for Finals.

有兩種方式：1）參加四大滿貫賽事累積積分，排名前16可進入香港總決賽；2）在嘉年華的黑馬挑戰賽中奪冠，直接獲得總決賽資格。

How to publish projects in Game Market?

如何在遊戲市場發布項目？

First, at least 1 core team member must be HKAIGA graduate, submit complete Game Design Document and playable Demo, pass community preliminary review to launch IGO crowdfunding. The entire process takes about 2-3 months.

首先需要核心團隊至少有1名HKAIGA畢業生，提交完整的遊戲設計文檔和可玩Demo，通過社區初審後即可啟動IGO眾籌。整個流程約2-3個月。

What is the academy employment rate?

學院的就業率如何？

We promise 95% employment rate (within 6 months of graduation). Graduates have three pathways: entrepreneurship (Game Market IGO), professional esports (team contracts), industry employment (100+ partner companies), with average starting salary of $60K/year.

我們承諾95%的就業率（畢業6個月內）。畢業生有三大出路：自主創業（遊戲市場IGO）、職業電競（戰隊簽約）、行業就業（100+合作企業），平均起薪$60K/年。

What are the uses of AIG tokens?

AIG代幣有什麼用途？

AIG is the universal token of the entire ecosystem, usable for: entry fee payment, community governance voting, academy tuition, game market investment, NFT trading, staking dividends, etc. Using AIG for payment enjoys discount benefits.

AIG是整個生態系統的通用代幣，可用於：參賽費用支付、社區治理投票、學院學費、遊戲市場投資、NFT交易、質押分紅等。使用AIG支付可享受折扣優惠。

How can virtual audiences participate?

虛擬觀眾如何參與？

Through the 404.world metaverse platform, global players can participate in Carnival and Championship with virtual identities. Virtual tickets cost only $10, support multi-view watching, real-time interactive voting, and earn AIG token rewards while watching.

通過404.world元宇宙平台，全球玩家可以虛擬身份參與嘉年華和冠軍賽。虛擬門票僅$10，支持多視角觀賽、實時互動投票，觀看即可獲得AIG代幣獎勵。
